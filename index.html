Charcoal Briquettes
Our Products

Our Charcoal Briquettes are made from Selected Granular Coconut Shells which are processed and printed using natural binders so that they become fuel with superior heat and last longer than ordinary charcoal and provide a distinctive aroma every time they burn. Bio Coco briquettes with the best selected ingredients from selected coconut charcoal are suitable for cooking, as body warmers, big events on New Year's holidays or Christmas, with long heat resistance.
